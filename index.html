WhatsApp AI Calendar Agent

Developed a WhatsApp AI Scheduling Assistant that automates meeting coordination using n8n, Google Calendar, and OpenRouter's LLMs. The AI agent listens for scheduling requests in WhatsApp, checks availability in Google Calendar, and either confirms the event or suggests alternatives — all without manual input. By leveraging low-code automation and AI, this project showcases how intelligent agents can streamline everyday tasks, reducing friction in scheduling and enabling broader automation in CRM, onboarding, and beyond.
